The tidy dataset is a merged dataset from the training and test sets achieved through the run_analysis.R script. It shows records for the activities WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING,performed by 30 volunteers (represented in this dataset as Subjects 1 to 30). The dataset shows observations for:  
- Triaxial(X,Y and Z) acceleration from the accelerometer (total acceleration) and the estimated body acceleration. 
- Triaxial (X,Y and Z) Angular velocity from the gyroscope. 
- A 561-feature vector with time and frequency domain variables.
